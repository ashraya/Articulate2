Rules for the Kids Version of the Game

Preparing to Play

  • Scan the QR Code, with your phone, in the Side Column to Access the Cards
  • Split the members Playing the game into two teams - Each turn team members are nominated DESCRIBERS and GUESSERS. All players MUST take their turn at being describers. No matter how big the team, there are never more than two describers.
  1. Teams of 2 players have 1 describer and 1 guesser
  2. Teams of 3 players have 2 describers and 1 guesser
  3. Teams of 4 players have 2 describers and 2 guessers

Playing the Game

  • Click the Start Button on your phone to start the timer for this turn. Make sure only the Describer can see the phone screen.
  1. The Screen will have a word corresponding to the category written on the square that the team's marker is on. Both teams will start with words from the object category.
  • The describer then needs to describe the word on the screen
  • When a team member shouts out the correct answer the describers quickly click the next word button and repeat the process
  • When the timer runs out, the turn is finished. Your team's marker will move (depending on how many words you guessed correctly) on the larger screen. Play will then pass onto the next team
  • Describers must be careful not to break any of the description rules (see below). If they break one of these rules they forfeit the turn.
  1. If the turn is forfeited wait for the timer to run out on your turn before passing onto the next team.
  • The first team to reach 56 points wins!

Description rules

  • When describing you MUST NOT:
  1. Say what letter the word starts with, or how many letters it has.
  2. Say the word or any derivative eg. if the word is "post" you may not say "postage" or "postman"; for "swim" you may not say "swimmer" etc.
  3. Use "rhymes with" or "sounds like" type clues.
  • When describing YOU MAY:
  1. Choose to PASS and not play a card
  2. Gesticulate, act, mime (NOT mouth the word).
